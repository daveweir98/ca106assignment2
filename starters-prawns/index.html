Prawn Dish

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • crushed chilli flakes to taste
  • 700g fresh king prawns

Recipe

  1. Add the olive oil, lemon juice, parsley, garlic and black pepper to a large glass bowl and stir together. Also add crushed chilli if wanted.
  2. Add prawns to the bowl and toss to coat
  3. Refridgerate for 30 minutes
  4. Heat the barbacue at high heat.
  5. Thread the prawns onto skewers
  6. Lightly oil the cooking grate and cook prawns for 2-3 minutes

Information and Allergies

Care must be taken when preparing this dish as it is easy to burn yourself while the barbacue is at a high heat. This dish should not be prepared if you or anyone you wish to serve it to is allergic to any of the ingredients listed above.
